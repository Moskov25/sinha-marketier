Our Story - Sinha Marketier

“I’ve always been someone who picks up skills fast. I learned design, content, ads, funnels, SEO — not because someone told me to, but because I was curious. I enjoyed building things and figuring out how to make them grow.”

Coming from a tech background, I knew how to execute. But when it came to jobs, something never clicked. Despite learning all sides of digital marketing — from content to strategy — the calls never came. The big brands never looked past the resume.

I wasn’t from a top college. I didn’t have referrals. But I had something they didn’t see: a full-stack marketing mindset and a hunger to build.

So instead of waiting around, I started my own thing.

That’s how Sinha Marketier came to life — just me and two close friends from college — one who’s now at Amazon, and the other who’s chosen to stay lowkey.

We started this with a clear vision:
To help businesses — especially growing brands — understand how to scale online, the right way.

No fluff, no shortcuts. Just real strategy, clean execution, and consistent results.

We work in three clear marketing tiers, crafted to help companies at different stages. You’ll find them on our homepage. If our story resonates with you — whether you’re a founder, startup team, or just someone trying to grow online —
